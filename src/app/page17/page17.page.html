Pellea wrightiana

Wright's Cliffbreak

Description-Leaves dark green to bluish green, long and arrow, with shining purple stem. Lower leaflets form clusters of five or more (truly bipinnate) and plants may be shrubby.

Spores-sori concealed beneath the reflexed margins.

Habitat-Full sun, rocky slopes and cliffs, 3,000-8,000 ft

Range-
