Pteridium aquilinum

Bracken fern

Description- winter deciduous. Large roughly triangular fronds. Grows 1-3 feet tall. There is only one branch from the base, and the broadly triangular blades are tri-pinnate

Spores - Sporangia are formed in sori on the underside of the frond.They are arranged in narrow brown bands, and form spores over July, August and September.

Habitat - pastures, deciduous and coniferous woodlands and hillsides.

Range - native to Europe, East Asia, and North America.
