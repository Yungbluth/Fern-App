Asplenium trichomanes

Maidenhair Spleenwart

Description - grows 1-3 inches tall. Evergreen fronds are long and narrow, gradually tapering towards the tip. Roundish pinnae. Stipe and rachis are dark along the length.

Spores - linear to oval, straight, and attached to the upper-side of the fertile vein. There are 4 to 8 sori per pinna.

Habitat-rocky habitats such as cliffs, cree slopes, walls, and mine waste. Sea level up to 10,000ft.

Range - worldwide
