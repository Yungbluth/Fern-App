Myriopteris lindheimeri

Fairy Swords

Description - rounded above; blade oblong-lanceolate to ovate-deltate, Broad triangular grayish-green tripinnate leaves have a rough sandpapery feel. Leaflets covered in coarse dense white hair on top, tan hair underneath with light brown sales on veins.

Spores -False indusia marginal, weakly differentiated.sporulating summer-fall

Habitat - rocky slopes and ledges, on a variety of acidic to mildly basic substrates from 1,000-8,500 ft

Range - Ariz., N.Mex., Tex.; Mexico
